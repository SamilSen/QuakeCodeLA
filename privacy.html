QuakeCode – Privacy Policy 

Effective Date: July 21, 2025 

This Privacy Policy applies to the latest version of QuakeCode, our mobile application 
dedicated to AI-powered earthquake prediction. It explains how we collect, use, store, and 
protect user data. By using QuakeCode, you agree to the practices described below. 

 

1. Information We Collect 

QuakeCode may collect the following types of information: 



 

Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and general system 
performance data to ensure compatibility, security, and proper functionality. 



 

Location Data: Only collected with your explicit consent, for delivering location-
specific earthquake predictions and alerts. 



 

App Usage Data: Data on how you use the app, including frequency, duration, and 
feature interactions, to help us improve the app. 



 

User Feedback and Reports: Voluntary input from users, such as felt earthquake reports 
or feedback about the app’s functionality. 

 

2. How We Use Your Data 

We use collected data to: 



 

Enhance the accuracy and performance of our earthquake prediction algorithms. 



 

Provide personalized, location-based alerts and insights (if location permission is 
granted). 



 

Improve and customize your user experience. 



 

Identify and resolve technical issues or bugs. 



 

Support anonymized scientific research aimed at improving earthquake forecasting 
models. 

 

3. Data Sharing 



 

No Personal Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with 
advertisers or third parties for commercial purposes. 




 

Research Collaboration: We may share anonymized, aggregated data with academic 
and scientific partners to contribute to earthquake science and AI model development. 

 

4. Data Storage and Deletion 



 

Secure Storage: Your data is stored using industry-standard encryption and security 
practices to prevent unauthorized access or misuse. 



 

Local Data: Uninstalling the app will automatically delete all data stored locally on your 
device. 



 

Server Data: You may request the permanent deletion of any data stored on our servers 
by contacting us. 

 

5. Your Privacy Rights 

As a QuakeCode user, you have the right to: 



 

Know what data we collect and how we use it. 



 

Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. 



 

Revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. 



 

Contact us regarding any privacy-related questions or concerns. 

 

6. Changes to This Policy 

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or app features. 
Significant updates will be communicated through the app or official channels.
